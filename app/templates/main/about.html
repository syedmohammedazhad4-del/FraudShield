About FraudShield

FraudShield is an intelligent insurance fraud detection system that leverages machine learning to help insurance companies identify and prevent fraudulent claims in real-time, saving millions in financial losses.

The Problem

Insurance fraud costs the global industry an estimated $80 billion annually. In automobile insurance alone, fraudulent claims account for 5-10% of all claims, leading to inflated premiums for genuine policyholders. Traditional manual review processes are slow, inconsistent, and unable to detect sophisticated fraud patterns hidden across thousands of claims.

Our Solution

FraudShield uses the AdaBoost Classifier — an ensemble machine learning algorithm that combines multiple weak classifiers to form a strong, accurate predictive model. The system analyzes 25 key attributes of each insurance claim, including policy details, accident circumstances, vehicle information, and claimant behavior patterns to detect anomalies that indicate fraud. Combined with SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique) to handle class imbalance, the model achieves robust fraud detection even when fraudulent cases represent less than 6% of all claims.

How It Works
1
Input Claim Data

Analysts enter claim details through a guided 4-step wizard — policy info, personal details, vehicle data, and claim specifics.

2
Data Processing

Human-readable inputs are automatically encoded and standardized to match the model's training format.

3
ML Analysis

The AdaBoost model evaluates the claim against learned fraud patterns from 15,000+ historical records.

4
Verdict & Score

A Fraud or Legitimate verdict is returned with a confidence percentage for informed decision-making.

Key Features
Real-Time Detection

Instant fraud analysis with confidence scoring. No waiting — results appear within seconds of submission.

Role-Based Access

Admins oversee all company activity. Analysts manage their own predictions. Secure, role-based system.

Policy Validation

Claims without valid policy numbers (POL prefix) are automatically flagged as fraud — zero tolerance.

Performance Analytics

Comprehensive model metrics — Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, AUC-ROC, confusion matrix, and feature importance.

Audit Trail

Every prediction is logged with timestamp, policy number, user, and confidence. Full traceability for compliance.

Responsive Design

Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Clean, minimal interface designed for ease of use.
